About

I am currently a Research Machine Learning Scientist at Layer 6, where I work on predictive modeling, in particular tabular foundation models. I received my PhD from the University of Groningen under the supervision of Christian Hirsch. The title of my dissertation is Limit theory for spatial random networks.

I was a post-doc at Toronto Metropolitan University. Until last month, while at TMU, I was also a research intern at Mastercard through a Mitacs Elevate grant focused on fraud detection in financial transaction networks.

I also love basketball and am a passionate skier.

  • PhD University of Groningen - limit theory for spatial random networks.

  • Current Research Machine Learning Scientist at Layer 6, focused on predictive modeling and tabular foundation models.
